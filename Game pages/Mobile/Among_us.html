Find the imposter... before it's too late!

Among Us is a very popular game that has gained a lot of popularity recently. It's a multiplayer game where players are assigned the role of either a crewmate or an imposter. The crewmates' goal is to complete tasks on the spaceship while trying to identify and vote off the imposter. On the other hand, the imposter's goal is to kill all the crewmates without getting caught. It's a game that requires strategy, communication, and teamwork.

Minimun requirements

Operating System: Among Us is compatible with Android 6.0 and later, as well as iOS 13.0 and later.

Storage Space: The game requires approximately 150 MB of free space on your device.

RAM: It is recommended to have at least 1 GB of RAM on your device to play the game smoothly.

Internet Connection: Among Us is an online multiplayer game, so a stable internet connection is required to play the game.

Screen Size: The game can be played on any device with a minimum screen size of 4.5 inches.

Graphics: The game features simple graphics, so it should run smoothly on most devices.
