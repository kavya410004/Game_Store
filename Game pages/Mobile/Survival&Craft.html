«Hey, survivor! Watch out, shark is here!»

A terrible plane crash took the lives of all the passengers and crew... except for one. You a survivor and now you are out of civilization. Around the ocean, hot sun and hungry shark. An almost hopeless situation and a huge opportunity to die throw adrenaline into your blood. You have only one thing — craft for survive.

Minimun requirements

Operating system: The game is compatible with both Android and iOS operating systems.

Storage space: The game requires a minimum of 100 MB of free storage space on your mobile device.

RAM: The game requires at least 1 GB of RAM to run smoothly.

Processor: The game can be played on devices with at least a dual-core processor. However, it is recommended to have a quad-core processor for better performance.

Graphics: The game requires a device with at least Adreno 320 or Mali-400 MP graphics card to run smoothly.

It is always recommended to check the official game requirements before downloading the game to ensure optimal performance.
