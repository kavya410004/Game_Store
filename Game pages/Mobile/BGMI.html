Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a popular battle royale game developed by Krafton.In BGMI, players are air-dropped onto a deserted island with up to 100 other players. They then have to scavenge for weapons, ammunition, and supplies while avoiding enemy players. The ultimate goal is to be the last player or team standing by eliminating all other players or surviving until the end of the game.

Minimun requirements

Operating System: Android 5.1.1 or above

RAM: 2 GB or more

Storage: 2 GB or more of free storage space

Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 430, MediaTek MT6737, or equivalent or higher

Internet Connection: Stable internet connection (Mobile Data or Wi-Fi)

Note that these are the minimum requirements, and the game may perform better on devices with higher specifications.
