Explore large, destructible environments where no two games are ever the same. Team up with friends by sprinting, climbing and smashing your way to earn your Victory Royale, whether you choose to build up in Fortnite Battle Royale or go no-builds in Fortnite Zero Build.

Minimun requirements

Operating System: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit or Mac OS X Sierra (10.12.6+)

Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent

Memory: 4 GB RAM

Graphics Card: Intel HD 4000 or equivalent, with DirectX 11 support

Storage: 15 GB available space

Keep in mind that these are the minimum requirements, and meeting them may result in lower graphics settings and decreased performance.
